This website, Alnitak, is an unofficial practice tool designed for CharaChorder 3D input devices, including CharaChorder One, CharaChorder Two and Master Forge.

Page Introductions

Key Lesson Page

At key lesson page, you can practice typing the number, letter, or symbol keys on your CharaChorder 3D input device. The accessible lessons are listed in the side menu.

Statistics Page

At statistics page, you can check the statistics of your practices in key lessons, including a graph that shows your typing speed and combo counts over time.

Chord Page

At chord page, you can pick several chords in your chord library and practice them.

Layout Viewer Page

At layout page, you can see the actual layout of your device under a certain OS keyboard layout and shift state. It also provides key searching and layout printing features.

Settings Page

At settings page, you can tweak some settings of this tool, such as the visibility of some components, the highlight logic on the layout preview, the device layout, the air mode, and the chord library.
